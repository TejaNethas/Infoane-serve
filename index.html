COMPANY FOUNDERS

STEVE GOLDNER

Head – US Operations

Steve has more than 30 years of operational experience and has served as Vice President of Engineering and Operations at a number of startups and public companies. Steve has extensive experience in development, quality assurance, IT and data center operations. Steve has participated in raising venture capital, IPOs as well as a number of mergers and acquisitions. He served as Vice President Engineering and Operations for Selectica, Active Decisions (acquired by Knova), Cascadeworks (acquired by Elance), i2 Technologies, Supplybase (acquired by i2), Diffusion, Worldtalk (IPO), Polycom and Sun Microsystems (IPO).

Steve is a co-founder of Cresta Partners, an innovative executive search and consulting firm focused on early to mid-stage technology companies. He has a MSEE and a BSEE from the University of Michigan.

 

MOHAN DUGGI

Head – India Operations

Mohan has over 20 years of experience in the Financial, Insurance, Retail and Wholesale sectors with an emphasis on project management and Delivery management. Prior to Infoane, Mohan worked for Virtusa, Active Decisions Inc. USA, and Polaris Software Lab. He has extensive experience in onshore/offshore team management, Data modeling, Database Architect/Administration, Data Warehousing tools and reporting systems. He has designed and managed very large production, development and data warehousing databases in 24x7 environment.

Mohan worked on many prestigious client projects, which includes Johnson and Johnson, JP Morgan Chase and CitiStreet (A financial organization).

Mohan is active in many charitable organizations. He graduated in Computer Science from Bharathidasan University.
